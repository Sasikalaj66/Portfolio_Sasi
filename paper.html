Paper 1

Paper on “Power Generation from Dance Floor” in SPAVE – 2K22 at Kongu Engineering College.

The pressure energy that is produced due to the walking or dancing on the floor is converted into mechanical energy by using rack and pinion mechanism.Then it is used to produce power by using dynamo.
